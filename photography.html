PHOTOGRAPHY COMPETITION – CENTHORA'25

Rules & Regulations

Topic: Capturing the Clues:A Forensic Story Through the Lens

  1. Photos must capture forensic science-related themes (e.g., mystery, clues, investigation).
  2. Participants can submit only 1 photograph(Submission link will be provided later).
  3. Photos should be original and taken by the participant
  4. Digital photos must be submitted in JPEG format with good resolution.
  5. Editing is allowed but must not alter the original content drastically.
  6. Photos should be appropriate for all audiences.
  7. Submit photos with captions explaining the concept behind each image.
  8. Photos will be judged on creativity, storytelling, composition, and relevance.
  9. Only individual submissions are allowed.
  10. Top 3 will be announced trough our official pages.
